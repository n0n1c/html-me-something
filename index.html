Nong and Emily's Kitchen Renovation Project

This is the status of our attempt to renovate our kitchen with very little previous construction experience.(We still have all our fingers!)

We recently bought our first home

The house was structurally very sound, but every inch of the house needed a cosmetic upgrade.

We began by painting the walls and ceilings in one of the bedrooms and the living room

The first real down to the studs renovation was the kitchen

Kitchen Walls

Half way up the kitchen walls were covered with two layers of tile, a two inch deep layer of mortar and a metal grate.

Removal of these tiles involved using a hammer drill to take off the top layer of tiles.

The next layer of tiles were removed using a hammer to chip both the tiles and mortar into smaller pieces

The metal grate was then pulled off the walls with gloves on, because the edges were razor sharp.

We then installed shiplap on the walls

We also had to install new electric outlets and move one existing outlet.
